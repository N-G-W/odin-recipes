Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A staple food across most of the americas and europe, the grilled cheese is not only the easiest thing to make it is also the most relaxing comfort food for most people. It is a simple recipe with a slice or a couple of slices of different cheeses if you are fancy, between some good old bread and toasted till crisp.

Paired with a nice dip or tomato soup is one of the best meals on the planet.

Ingredients

  • Cheese(mozzarella,cheddar, parmesian,etc.)
  • Bread(any)

Steps

  1. Assemble the sandwich
  2. Butter a pan and toast each side for couple of minutes until cheese is melted
  3. Use weights or spatula to press the bread and make sure the cheese melts evenly
