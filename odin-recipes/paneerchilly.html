Paneer Chilly

Crispy and spicy sauted paneer with some vegetables is one of the best ways to consume cottage cheese. If your a vegan tired of cottage cheese this might make you have a change of heart.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Cottage Cheese)
  • Vegetables(Onions, garlic any other vegetable of choosing)
  • Spices(Salt, pepper, cumin, other preferable spices)

Steps

  1. Add oil in a pan
  2. Stir fry the chillies
  3. Add spices
  4. Add some soy sauce
  5. Add paneer and stir fry till the paneer is covered and desired texture is achieved
