Rajma

A classic dish in Nepali cuisine that consists of beans that are cooked to perfection, and sauted with a little bit of spices, this dish is best served with a bowl of rice and maybe garnished with onions.

Ingredients

  • Red kidney beans
  • Spices: salt, cumin, turmeric,pepper,ginger,
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Put the beans in a pressure cooker
  2. Add water and cook the beans
  3. Remove the cooked beans from the cooker and place in a bowl
  4. Add 2-3 spoons of oil or ghee to a pan
  5. Add onions and saute till transparent
  6. Add tomatoes and cook till mushy
  7. Add 1-2 spoonful of spices depending on the amount of beans
  8. Cook till the vegtable mixture is mushy and dehydrated
  9. Add a little bit of the cooked beans
  10. Slowly keep adding and incorporating the beans with moderate stirring and occasionally smash some beans for texture
